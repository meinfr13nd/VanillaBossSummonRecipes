Vanilla Boss Summon Recipes adds summon items and crafting recipes for vanilla bosses/events/weather that do not have summons as of 1.4.7 . The events/bosses/weather are listed below see README at https://github.com/meinfr13nd/VanillaBossSummonRecipes for more details

Events: 
  - Slime Rain (Slim Bucket)
  - Martian Invaders (Orphaned Alien)
  - Spawn Meteor (Mock Meteor)

Bosses:
  - Wall of Flesh
  - Queen Slime
  - Golem
  - Duke Fishron
  - Empress of Light
  - Skeletron

Weather:
  - Rain (Rain Stick)
  - Sandstorm (Sandy Rain Stick)
