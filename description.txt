Vanilla Boss Summon Recipes adds summon items and crafting recipes for vanilla bosses/events that do not have summons as of 1.4.7 . The events/bosses are listed below see README at https://github.com/meinfr13nd/VanillaBossSummonRecipes for more details

Events: 
  - Slime Rain
  - Martian Invaders
  - Spawn Meteor

Bosses:
  - Wall of Flesh
  - Queen Slime
  - Golem
  - Duke Fishron
  - Empress of Light
  - Skeletron
